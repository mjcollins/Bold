Lifetime Value Methodology

My suggestion is to use a three-year window across all donor types and add up four main things:

  • the initial gift when they sign on,
  • any renewals they have already committed to,
  • extra funding added later (for example, if they expand their scholarship),
  • and referral revenue—money that came in because they brought someone else along.

All of this is pulled from our pipeline data (2022–2024) and matched against what finance actually recorded.

In plain terms: LTV = Initial Gift + Renewal Commitments + Expansion Funding + Referral Revenue.

What we’re assuming here:

  • Renewals are based on signed intent letters.
  • Expansion means extra funding that kicks in after year one.
  • Referrals capture donations that get credited back to the original donor’s influence.
